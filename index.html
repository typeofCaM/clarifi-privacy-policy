Clarifi — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

What Clarifi Does

Clarifi is a family budget tracking app that helps households log spending, manage shopping trips, and stay on top of monthly budgets together in real time.

What Data We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address from your Google account, used solely for authentication and displaying your identity within your household.
  • Spending data: Transaction amounts, categories, notes, and shopping trip details that you voluntarily enter into the app.
  • Household data: Household name, member list, budget targets, and invite codes used to link family members.
  • Device tokens: Push notification tokens stored to deliver budget alerts to your device.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore (part of Google Firebase), hosted in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest per Google Cloud's standard security practices.

Who Can Access Your Data

Only members of your household can see your household's spending data. Each household is isolated — no other Clarifi users can view your transactions, budgets, or shopping trips. The app developer (Cameron Patch) has administrative access to the Firebase project for maintenance and support purposes only.

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal data with any third parties. Clarifi contains no advertising and no analytics tracking beyond basic Firebase usage metrics.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below and we will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Clarifi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
